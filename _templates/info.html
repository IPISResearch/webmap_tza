Interactive Map of Mining sites in Tanzania

Overview

The present map is an interactive webmap of the mining sites in Tanzania.

How to use this map

In the legend on the right, you can select the data set that you wish to make visible on the map. You can also create your own custom maps by switching layers in the legend on or off to overlay them. Within many layers, you can also switch certain elements comprising that data layer on or off. In this way, you can for example filter by mineral, mine ype, etc.

You can easily save or share any of your custom maps by copying the unique URL address.
